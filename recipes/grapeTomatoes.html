Grape Tomatoes

Ingredients

  • 1 pound grape tomatoes, halved
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 5 fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh thyme
  • salt to taste

Directions

Step 1

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

Step 2

Place tomatoes onto a large square of aluminum foil. Drizzle olive oil
over tomatoes and top with garlic, basil, thyme, and salt. Wrap the
foil around tomato mixture sealing tightly to keep juices inside.

Step 3

Bake in the preheated oven until tomatoes are tender, about 30 
minutes. Cool slightly.
